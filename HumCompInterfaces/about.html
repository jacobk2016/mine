What is Simply Social?

Welcome to Simply Social!


Simply social is a web interface designed to help adults of all ages learn more about how to use the internet for social activities. We are a non-profit organization with a focus on helping you learn.


This website is made up of tutorials which can teach you how to use web browsers, email, and a few common social networks. Our tutorials are written by an experienced team, with your needs in mind.


Simply Social was originally designed for beginners, but our tutorials extend beyond beginner concepts. You can learn more about your favorite online social tools than you knew before simply by selecting a subject matter you are interested in and browsing through the tutorial.


Our team is dedicated to proving a quality learning experience for you. If you have any questions, comments or concerns, you can submit a message through our feedback form. If you prefer another method of communication, you can also check out our contact page.


Thank you for visiting our website and using our tutorials. Come back soon!
