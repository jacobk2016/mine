Email Lessons (Basics)

Send an Email


In this section, you will learn how to send an email. You'll need to begin by logging into your email account. If you haven't created one, that's okay. You can just read it for now and come back to this lesson again later if you need to.


After you log into your email account, you'll be on the inbox screen. Somewhere on the screen, usually in the upper lefthand corner, you'll see a "new message" or "create message" or "compose" type of button. This button will open a window for you to create a new email. Go ahead and click on it.


Below, you'll find a diagram of the email composition window. In case you forgot the parts, we went ahead and re introduced a few from the "Email Interface" lesson. If you scroll down past the diagram, you'll see the parts labled with instructions.





1. This is your "To" line. You'll first need to type in the email address of the person you want to send your email to. For example, "simplysocialtutorials@yahoo.com". To send it to multiple people, you'll want to seperate the email address with a ", " like so: "happy@gmail.com, auntbarbara@yahoo.com, mel123@hotmail.com". Don't include the quotation marks in your "To" line! Just type it like this, without them: happy@gmail.com


2. This is your subject line. Write a brief, purposeful phrase that describes what the email is about.


3. This is the content section. Click the blank space and write your message here.


4. This is the send button. Double check your "To" line, your Subject line and content for accuracy. Now, click send.


That's all it takes! Sending an email is just as quick as filling out the blanks and clicking a button. In the next section, you're going to learn about email attachments.
