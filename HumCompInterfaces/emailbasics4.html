Email Lessons (Basics)

Set up a Contacts List


Setting up a contacts list is like having an address book full of email addresses. It makes it easier to keep track of all your commonly used email addresses for the people you email the most.

After you're logged into your email account, you'll want to start by clicking on the "contact list" button. The "contact list" button looks a little different in every interface, but in this example it's a small icon in the upper left corner. Sometimes you will see a folder in the folder section that is called "contacts". Below, we've provided a diagram of where to find your contact list button.





After you click on the contacts button, a screen sort of like the one in the diagram below will appear. The next step is to click on "Create Contact". You can find this button at the end of the red arrow in the diagram.



After you click on the "Create Contact" button, a screen sort of like the one in the diagram below should appear.





You can fill out the blanks with whatever information is important to you. Not all of it is required, so just put what you like. The most important part is to make sure that the email address is exactly correct. That way, you don't accidentally send emails using the contact you created, and have those emails go to the wrong person.

When you're done filling out all the lines in the form that you want, click "Save". The "Save" button will not appear until you have typed in contact information in at least one name field. You can see the "Save" button pointed out in the above diagram, where it is circled in red.



After you have saved a contact, you can see them in your contacts list every time you click on your "Contacts" button like we did before. You can see an example of how this might look below.



Now that you know how to create contacts, it's time for you to learn how to send an email. To move on click "Next Lesson" below, or to review the Folders lesson, you can click "Previous Lesson".
